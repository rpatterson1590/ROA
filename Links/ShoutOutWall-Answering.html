Myles Christian (Answering Adventism)

Myles Christian, widely known as "Answering Adventism" on Youtube, is a former third-generation Adventist turned evangelical apologist dedicated to defending biblical truth and exposing doctrinal errors in Seventh-day Adventism.

Through articles, debates, and concise video content, Myles uses Scripture, historical context, and sound reasoning to challenge misleading teachings and strengthen believers’ faith.

With candid clarity and unwavering conviction, he bridges deep theology and everyday conversation—not for fame, but to equip Christians to discern truth and stand firm in Christ.
