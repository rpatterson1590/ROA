About Rob of Atlanta

Welcome to Rob of Atlanta! I’m still and will forever be a student of apologetics. and creator based in West Metro Atlanta. Here I share theological insights, historical research, and personal reflections on faith, family, and culture.

I'm a fellow pilgrim on the Christian journey who still has far more to learn than to teach. My aim isn’t to champion one denomination over another or to settle every doctrinal dispute—it’s simply to walk alongside brothers and sisters from every tradition, asking honest questions and pointing us all back to the core of the Gospel. I won’t pretend to hold every answer, but I’m passionate about creating a space where we can explore Scripture, history, and reason together—without shutting anyone out—so that, united in humility and love, we can grow ever closer to the truth we share in Christ.
