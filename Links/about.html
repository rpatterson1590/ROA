About Rob of Atlanta

"For I determined to know nothing among you except Jesus Christ, and Him crucified." - St. Paul the Apostle (1 Corinthians 2:2)

Welcome to Rob of Atlanta! I’m still and will forever be a student of apologetics and creator based in West Metro Atlanta. Here I share theological insights, historical research, and personal reflections on faith, family, and culture.

“It is better to be a Christian without saying so, than to say one is a Christian and not be one.” - St. Ignatius of Antioch

I'm a fellow pilgrim on the Christian journey who still has far more to learn than to teach. My aim isn’t to champion one denomination over another... it’s simply to walk alongside brothers and sisters from every tradition, asking honest questions and pointing us all back to the core of the Gospel.
