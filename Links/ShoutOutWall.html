Dwayne Bradford (Debunking Hebrew Israelites)

Dwayne Bradford, widely known as “Dwayne Debunking Hebrew” on TikTok, is a bold Christian voice dedicated to defending biblical truth and exposing false doctrine—particularly the misinformation spread by the Hebrew Israelite movement.

Through short-form video content, Dwayne uses Scripture, historical context, and sound logic to challenge misleading claims and help believers stand firm in their faith.

With a streetwise clarity and unapologetic delivery, Dwayne bridges the gap between deep theology and real-world conversations. He’s not out for clout—he’s out for truth, and he’s passionate about equipping others to see through spiritual deception with the Word of God as their weapon.

Whether you're wrestling with identity-based heresy or just curious about biblical accuracy, Dwayne’s content cuts through the noise and points back to Christ.
