Fatheer Josiah B. Trenham

Father Josiah Trenham is an Archpriest in the Antiochian Orthodox Christian Archdiocese, serving as pastor of St. Andrew Orthodox Church in Riverside, California since 1998. He was ordained in 1993, earned a Ph.D. in Theology from the University of Durham (UK) in 2004, and teaches theology and history at various academic institutions.

Known for founding Patristic Nectar Publications and hosting over 700 homilies on his podcast The Arena and YouTube channel Patristic Nectar Films, he’s a prolific publisher and conference speaker.

Trenham and his wife Catherine have been married since 1988 and raised ten children, and he continues to mentor through roles such as chaplain for Orthodox Christians for Life and St. Andrew Academy.

A voice of influence and occasional controversy, he garners praise for bold, unapologetic teaching—while some criticize his firm stance on pastoral issues and cultural commentary.

Nonetheless, he remains a respected and canonically-recognized priest whose media outreach has significantly shaped English-speaking Orthodoxy today.

Sam Shamoun– The Lion with the Mic (Halal Hogan)

Sam Shamoun is one of the most aggressive voices in Christian apologetics—especially when it comes to Islam. He’s got decades of experience, a deep command of Scripture, and he doesn’t flinch when truth is on the line.

"Now let me be real—Sam’s language? Yeah, it’s wild sometimes. I can't co-sign every word that flies outta his mouth. But I’d be lying if I said I haven’t learned a lot from him. His breakdowns of Scripture, his bold stance against deception, and the way he stands firm for Christ—those have made an impact on me."

He’s not polished—but he’s powerful. If you can filter through the fire, you’ll find gold. From demolishing false doctrines to sharpening biblical theology, Sam’s work pushes you to dig deeper and stand taller in the faith.

You can find him on YouTube under Sam Shamoun, or teaching regularly on Answering Islam and Shamounian Apologetics.

Dwayne Bradford (Debunking Hebrew Israelites)

Dwayne Bradford, known online as “Dwayne Debunking Hebrew,” is a committed Christian apologist who specializes in exposing the theological errors of the Hebrew Israelite movement.

Through concise TikTok videos and almost-daily live sessions, Dwayne engages directly with viewers—calling out false teachings, fielding tough questions, and walking through Scripture in real time.

With solid historical context, clear exegesis, and unflinching conviction, he equips believers to spot and resist spiritual deception. He’s not after viral fame; he’s after a firm grasp of biblical truth that points back to Christ.

Myles Christian (Answering Adventism)

Myles Christian was born inside Seventh-day Adventism—literally third-generation—but now he’s its fiercest critic. On AnsweringAdventism.com and YouTube he unpacks Adventist teachings with a scholar’s precision and a pastor’s heart.

Whether it’s a deep-dive article or a point-by-point video debate, Myles layers verse-by-verse exegesis over historical context to dismantle errors and reinforce core gospel truths.

His delivery balances academic rigor and pastoral warmth: he’s not after clicks, he’s after clarity—helping fellow believers navigate tough doctrines so their faith isn’t built on sand.
