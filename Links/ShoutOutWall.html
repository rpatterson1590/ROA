Dwayne Bradford (“Dwayne Debunking Hebrew”)

Dwayne Bradford, known online as “Dwayne Debunking Hebrew,” is a committed Christian apologist who specializes in exposing the theological errors of the Hebrew Israelite movement.

Through concise TikTok videos and almost-daily live sessions, Dwayne engages directly with viewers—calling out false teachings, fielding tough questions, and walking through Scripture in real time.

With solid historical context, clear exegesis, and unflinching conviction, he equips believers to spot and resist spiritual deception. He’s not after viral fame; he’s after a firm grasp of biblical truth that points back to Christ.

Myles Christian (Answering Adventism)

Myles Christian (Answering Adventism)

Myles Christian was born inside Seventh-day Adventism—literally third-generation—but now he’s its fiercest critic. On AnsweringAdventism.com and YouTube he unpacks Adventist teachings with a scholar’s precision and a pastor’s heart.

Whether it’s a deep-dive article or a point-by-point video debate, Myles layers verse-by-verse exegesis over historical context to dismantle errors and reinforce core gospel truths.

His delivery balances academic rigor and pastoral warmth: he’s not after clicks, he’s after clarity—helping fellow believers navigate tough doctrines so their faith isn’t built on sand.
