Proofs That Jesus Is God Almighty

1. "I AM"

  • God: “I AM WHO I AM” (Exodus 3:14)
  • Jesus: “Before Abraham was, I AM” (John 8:58)

2. The First and the Last

  • God: “I am the first and I am the last” (Isaiah 44:6)
  • Jesus: “I am the First and the Last” (Revelation 1:17)

3. The Only Savior

  • God: “There is no savior besides Me” (Isaiah 43:11)
  • Jesus: “I am the way, the truth, and the life” (John 14:6)

4. The Forgiver of Sins

  • God: “I, even I, am the one who wipes out your transgressions” (Isaiah 43:25)
  • Jesus: “Son, your sins are forgiven” (Mark 2:5–10)

5. The Judge of All

  • God: “Shall not the Judge of all the earth do right?” (Genesis 18:25)
  • Jesus: “The Father has given all judgment to the Son” (John 5:22)

6. The Lord of the Sabbath

  • God: Creator and lawgiver of the Sabbath (Genesis 2:3; Exodus 20:10–11)
  • Jesus: “The Son of Man is Lord even of the Sabbath” (Mark 2:28)

7. The Shepherd

  • God: “The Lord is my Shepherd” (Psalm 23:1)
  • Jesus: “I am the good Shepherd” (John 10:11)

8. The Light

  • God: “The Lord is my light and my salvation” (Psalm 27:1)
  • Jesus: “I am the light of the world” (John 8:12)

9. The Creator

  • God: “In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth” (Genesis 1:1)
  • Jesus: “All things were made through Him” (John 1:3; Colossians 1:16)

10. The One Worthy of Worship

  • God: “You shall worship the Lord your God” (Deuteronomy 6:13)
  • Jesus: Worshiped multiple times (Matthew 14:33; Matthew 28:9, 17)
