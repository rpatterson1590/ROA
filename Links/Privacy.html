Privacy Policy

At RobOfAtlanta.com, your privacy is respected and protected. This website exists to provide Christian content, blog articles, apologetics resources, and links to external media. We aim to be transparent about what data (if any) is collected and how it's used.

1. Information We Collect

RobOfAtlanta.com does not actively collect personal information. We do not ask you to register, log in, or submit your name or email to access content.

However, we may collect basic anonymous data through tools like:

  • Google Analytics or similar, to see how the site is used

This data is non-identifying and may include:

  • Your browser type
  • Time spent on the site
  • Pages visited
  • General location based on IP (e.g., city/state)

2. External Links

We link to YouTube videos, blog articles, and outside websites. RobOfAtlanta.com is not responsible for the privacy policies or tracking practices of those third-party sites. Always review their policies before interacting with them.

3. No Tracking for Marketing

We do not use third-party ads, trackers, or email marketing. You will not receive marketing messages or be followed around the internet.

4. Cookies

Cookies may be used for basic functions such as:

  • Remembering your theme settings (if implemented)
  • Improving performance
  • Basic visitor tracking through analytics

You can disable cookies through your browser at any time.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the site changes significantly (such as adding user accounts or contact forms). Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
